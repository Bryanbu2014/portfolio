Introduction

This is a clean, fast virtual stock trading terminal built with Python and Streamlit.

Paper Trading Lab allows users to practice stock trading using live market prices without risking real money. Users get a virtual $100,000 starting capital, and all their trades, portfolios, and watchlists are saved to a cloud database.

Features

  • Secure Login: Private accounts so only you can access your portfolio.
  • Cloud Saves: Your balance, trades, and watchlists are backed up to a Supabase database. You can close the app and pick up right where you left off.
  • Live Data: Real-time stock prices fetched using Yahoo Finance (yfinance).
  • Portfolio Tracking: Automatically calculates your total shares and average buy prices.
  • Watchlist: Add and track live prices for your favorite stocks.
  • Trade Journal: Keeps a full history of every buy and sell order.

Tech Stack

  • UI & Backend: Streamlit
  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  • Market Data: yfinance
  • Data Handling: pandas
