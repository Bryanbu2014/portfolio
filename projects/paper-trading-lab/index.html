Introduction

The Paper Trading Lab is a risk-free, simulated market environment designed for testing and developing trading strategies. By leveraging real-time data, comprehensive fundamental analysis tools, and interactive dashboards, it provides an immersive platform for understanding market dynamics without the financial risk.

Key Features

  • Market Terminal: Experience real-time market data directly within your dashboard.
  • Research Integration: Full stock fundamentals, recent 7-day high/low price points, and curated news articles using yfinance.
  • Session Security: Mandatory single-session policies ensuring your data is protected from multiple concurrent logins.

Future Enhancements

We are continuously refining the Paper Trading Lab with plans to add more complex order types, backtesting modules with historical data, and AI-driven insights to evaluate the efficacy of user-defined trading models.
