About Me

I'm a Malaysian student at the Deggendorf Institute of Technology where I'm working towards a Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence. My passion lies in the field of Natural Language Processing, inspired by the rise of large language model (LLM). Throughout my academic journey, I find myself fascinated by the intricate ways in which LLMs can interpret, generate, and transform human language. Amazing, isn't it? This fascination has led me to seek out various projects related to the field.

Through my academic path and internship role at Bosch, I have built a robust toolkit in Python, Computer Vision, and Data Analysis with cloud-based solutions. During my internship, I specialized in cloud-based data pipelines to process large and real-time datasets. My work focused on generating insights for defect detection and designing interactive dashboards to visualize these results for industrial decision-making.

Building on top of this foundation, my Bachelor's thesis focuses on the application of Computer Vision for defect inspection. The thesis aims to bridge the gap between research and production-ready industrial applications.
